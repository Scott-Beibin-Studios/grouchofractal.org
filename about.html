Groucho Fractal is a science comedy liberation-ecotech stage performance hosted by inventor/artist/engineer, Scott Beibin. The interactive multimedia show is powered by audience members pedaling a bicycle, and features demonstrations of Beibin's inventions and other futuristic device prototypes.

Themes for the performances revolve around the transformation of society as we all prepare for a post-carbon-fuel future on Earth: 


 ... where the natural environment has become unpredictable, yet humans have begun to explore Space and settle offworld 


 … where resources are also scarce, yet strong education, effective communication, horizontal coordination and self-awareness are valued as the key tools for survival. 

A tour is being planned for sometime in 2024.
